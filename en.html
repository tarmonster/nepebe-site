Why does NEPEBE exist?

Electromagnetic technologies are shaping the future of energy, medicine, and industrial systems. Europe needs an independent, high-level research institute that develops these systems for the coming decades.

"Science knows no borders — but institutions must take root."

NEPEBE fulfills this role: a European scientific community built on open collaboration and a long-term research ecosystem.
