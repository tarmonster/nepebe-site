Why does NEPEBE exist?

Electromagnetic technologies are shaping the future of energy, medicine, and industrial systems. Europe needs an independent, high-level research institute to lead these developments.

“Science has no borders — but institutions must take root.”

NEPEBE fulfills this role: a European scientific community with open collaboration and long-term vision.

  • Independence — European-based neutral research framework
  • Openness — Cooperation with universities and industry
  • Long-term — Planning beyond 2030
  • Innovation — From prototype to real-world solutions

Research Areas

  • Electromagnetic energy transfer
  • Resonance-based systems
  • Intelligent field control
  • Laboratory infrastructure
  • Simulation environments
  • European collaboration
